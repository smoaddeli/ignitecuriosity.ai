Reimagining Learning in an AI-Enabled World

Ignite Curiosity Lab is a research-driven education technology startup with a product-innovation mandate, transforming K–12 education through innovative AI-powered learning solutions.

Our Mission

Learning to think and collaborate with AI is a fundamental skill for the future.

At the heart of our work is a simple belief: this skill is essential to developing curiosity, creativity, computational reasoning, and lifelong learning skills. Our mission is to empower students and teachers to become more capable, reflective, and inventive in an AI-enabled world, helping them learn, think, and create with AI, not just use it.

Our Approach

Designing New Learning Experiences

We explore how AI can redefine human learning, teaching, and capability development, designing new learning experiences and product paradigms based on what we discover. Our approach focuses on skill development, agency, and cognitive growth, rather than optimizing engagement or efficiency alone.

Human-Centered AI

We build AI-powered learning environments that help students develop not only academic mastery but also critical thinking, collaboration, and creative problem-solving skills.

Rooted in Research

This means integrating learning science principles and the latest advancements in generative AI with insights from educational research. We leverage AI as both a tool and a learning partner.

Our Work in Practice

At the Intersection of Research, Pedagogy, and Technology

Our interdisciplinary team of educators, researchers, and engineers works side by side to build, test, and prototype new interaction models. Our approach blends educational research with hands-on innovation: every prototype, product, and classroom experiment advances our understanding of how humans and AI can learn together.

Flagship Project: InsightAI Math

Our flagship product, InsightAI Math, is an adaptive math learning platform powered by Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and dynamic content generation. It personalizes the learning experience while keeping teachers at the center, supporting them with research-backed tools that enhance reasoning, creativity, and conceptual understanding.

These innovations—from helping teachers personalize instruction to enabling students to explore ideas through dialogue with AI—transform classrooms into environments of curiosity, exploration, and growth, reimagining what effective learning looks like in the 21st-century classroom.

We’re not just building edtech tools; we’re designing and shaping a new way for humans and AI to learn, think, and grow together.
