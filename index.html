Learning, Reimagined.

Ignite Curiosity Lab is a research-driven edtech startup exploring how AI can redefine human learning, creativity, and capability.

Beyond the Tool

We believe learning to *think* and *create* with AI is a fundamental skill for the future. Our mission is to empower students and teachers to become more inventive, reflective, and capable in an AI-enabled world.

A New Approach to Learning

Focus on Core Skills

We build AI environments that develop critical thinking, collaboration, and creative problem-solving—not just optimize engagement metrics.

AI as a Learning Partner

Grounded in learning science, our AI acts as both a tool and a collaborative partner to help students reason, create, and apply knowledge.

Research in Practice

Our interdisciplinary team of educators, researchers, and engineers builds and tests new learning models. Our flagship, InsightAI Math, empowers teachers with research-backed tools to personalize learning.

Something New is Coming.

We’re not just building edtech tools; we’re shaping a new way for humans and AI to learn together. We are currently in stealth mode, but we'd love to hear from you.
